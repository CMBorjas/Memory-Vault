# Wide Area Network (WAN)

## Technical Details
- **Scope:** Covers large geographical distances (cities, countries, continents).
- **Primary Tech:** Fiber optics, satellites, leased lines.
- **Key Issue:** Latency and signal attenuation.

---

> [!abstract] 🧠 Memory Anchor: Withering Ambrosia Newts
> **The Imagery:** 
> Picture giant, translucent newts with skin that looks like bruised, rotting lilies (**Ambrosia**). These newts are stretched so thin across the Atlantic that their skin is tearing (**Withering**). One end is clamped onto a server in Denver; the other is gasping for air in a London data center.
>
> **The Scent Anchor:** 
> Imagine the heavy, suffocatingly sweet smell of wilting funeral flowers mixed with the sharp, eye-watering ammonia of a reptile tank that hasn't been cleaned in months.
>
> **The Logic:** The *Withering* of the newt is your brain's trigger for **Signal Attenuation** (the signal gets weaker/withers as it travels further).
